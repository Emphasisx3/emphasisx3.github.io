What is Ergo?

Ergo launched in 2019 and is a next-generation smart contract platform. A programmable blockchain that ensure the economic freedom of ordinary people through secure, accessible, and decentralized financial tools.

Ergo draws on ten years of blockchain developement, complementing tried and tested principles with the best peer-reviewed academic research into cryptography.
